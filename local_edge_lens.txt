edge highlight functionality vs local edge lens (Tominski et al.)

- the edge highlight functionality is an *encode* interaction technique
in which the edges connected to nodes within the lens are simply encoded with a colour dissimilar to the 
ones that are not
- the local edge lens on the other hand is a *filter* interaction technique in which all edges that are
not connected to nodes within the lens are filtered out

--> ultimately, one could say that the edge highlight functionality *adds* something to the 
encoding while the local edge lens *removes* something from the encoding

- additionally, the local edge lens overlays the space around the lens with a semi-transparent
plane so as to further focus on the inside of the lens
- in the edge highlight functionality the outside of the lens is not altered at all
